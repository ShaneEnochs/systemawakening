*term "Essence"
  A universal energy currency extracted from defeated monsters. Used to purchase skills and items from the System Store.

*term "System"
  The mysterious supernatural framework that appeared during the Awakening, granting humanity Classes, Skills, and stat points. Its origin and purpose remain unknown.

*term "Mana"
  The internal energy reserve used to power skills and magical abilities. Regenerates slowly over time.

*term "Class"
  A role or archetype assigned by the System based on a person's actions and potential. Classes determine which skills are available and how stats grow.

*term "Solace"
  The last underground city, built by a coalition of surviving nations beneath a mountain range. One of the few places left where ordinary people can live safely.

*term "Malphas"
  A Tier-10 Demon Lord, one of the Seven Highest. Summoned during the Fall and believed to be responsible for the collapse of surface civilization.

*term "Awakening"
  The event approximately eight years ago when the System suddenly appeared across the entire planet, granting humans their first stat points and Classes overnight.
