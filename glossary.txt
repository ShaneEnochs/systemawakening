*term "People"
  The happiness and loyalty of your subjects. Too low and they revolt;
  too high and mob rule takes hold.

*term "Treasury"
  Your wealth and financial standing. Too low means bankruptcy;
  too high attracts resentment and conspiracy.

*term "Church"
  Your relationship with religious authority. Too low risks
  excommunication; too high leads to theocratic overreach.

*term "Nobility"
  Your standing among the aristocracy and gentry. Too low means
  isolation; too high invites conspiracy from above and anger from below.
