*title [Chapter 1] The Hargrove Institute

The Hargrove Institute for System Studies campus is surprisingly small. As you step down from the bus, you wonder if this might be some kind of satellite location away from the main campus. A few buildings. All of them well kept. From all the stories you've heard about this place, you thought it would be more... well, more.

Being one of the few hunter academies in the country, finding any photos of it was impossible. Blurred out in every satellite image, scrubbed from the internet entirely. The mystery just made the myth you'd built up in your head even bigger. You had imagined a castle-like structure that you would maybe have to cross an ancient lake to get to, then you'd go to a huge, bewitched dinner hall with floating candles and everyone would clap for this year's batch of new students. Maybe there would even be an old guy in a pointy hat giving a speech about not going into the forest or something.

Not a pointy hat in sight.

This was supposed to be the Harvard or Yale of System academies, but if you are being completely honest, this looks more like a community college.

Regardless, you're here, and you feel...
