*skill last_shield_standing [Legendary] "Last Shield Standing VI" 0
  [legendary]Legendary[/legendary]
  Every time you stood between others and danger, this skill took shape. 
  [b]Description:[/b] Designate a single threat: creature, force, or group. While active, that threat cannot advance past your position. Attacks aimed at allies behind you redirect to you; you teleport to intercept if necessary.
  [b]Choose one of three modes:[/b]
  [b]Sentinel:[/b] Your defensive capabilities quadruple, but your attack power is halved.
  [b]Bastion:[/b] You cannot attack, but you cannot be moved or displaced by any means.
  [b]Bulwark:[/b] You become invulnerable for 0.5x skill level seconds and then the skill ends.
  [b]Activation cost:[/b] 500 mana, with 10 mana per second upkeep.
  Switching modes costs an additional 50 mana.

*skill last_bastion [Legendary] "Last Bastion I" 0
  [legendary]Legendary[/legendary]
  Bastions have served the same purpose for thousands of years: they are the places where the desperate make their stand, where supplies outlast siege engines, where time itself becomes a weapon. This skill is the answer to that ancient need. After countless battles where hordes of monsters have pressed in and retreat meant death for those behind you, you have proven yourself willing and capable of holding the line and have been awarded this skill. 
  [b]Description:[/b] Creates an underground bunker at the safest location within 2 kilometers, chosen by the [System]. The entrance is a 5-meter stairwell lined with wards and runes to damage intruders. Below it, an enchanted tunnel ends at a reinforced blast door. The chamber beyond provides 10 square meters per person, stocked with food, water, recycled air, and medical supplies for six months.
  [b]Capacity:[/b] 10 per skill level. Only those you designate at the time of casting may enter and exit freely.
  The bunker draws from a separate and dedicated mana reserve that charges at the speed of your mana regeneration, but only when your personal mana pool is full. Allies may contribute their mana directly to the reserve, and mana stones may be used to add up to 1,000,000 mana per year.
  After six months, the bunker collapses. Any non-living contents disappear. All living inhabitants are teleported to a safe location above ground.
  [b]Activation cost: 2,000,000 mana from dedicated reserve only.
  [b]Cooldown:[/b] 1 year.

*skill final_moment [Legendary] "Final Moment III" 0
  [legendary]Legendary[/legendary]
  When your health drops below 25%, you automatically gain triple damage reduction and +50% movement speed. The next single attack that would normally kill you is negated, and your next attack does triple your normal damage.
  [b]Cooldown:[/b] 1 day.

*skill unbreakable [Legendary] "Unbreakable VII" 0
  [legendary]Legendary[/legendary]
  Gain +1% physical defense per ally currently within 50 meters up to a max of +50%. Gain bonus maximum health equal to 50% of your Body stat per person who depends on you, as tracked by your class (max 100 people). When an ally within 50 meters is killed, gain an additional +1% damage and defense for 60 seconds.
  [b]Cooldown:[/b] 1 day.

*skill legacy_of_protection [Epic] "Legacy of Protection VI" 0
  [epic]Epic[/epic]
  Any location where you have held ground against a threat during a conflict lasting more than one hour becomes permanently altered: corruption there spreads 50% more slowly, and monsters native to that area are moderately weakened. Any person who directly witnesses you take damage to protect another while at that location gains +1% to all stats permanently. If you die while actively shielding others, the location is marked for one year, and all crafting and healing performed there is twice as effective.
  Diameter of effect is 100x skill level meters.

*skill unwavering_conviction [Epic] "Unwavering Conviction VIII" 0
  [epic]Epic[/epic]

  [b]Cooldown:[/b] 1 day.

*skill intervention [Epic] "Intervention X" 0
  [epic]Epic[/epic]

  [b]Cooldown:[/b] 1 minute.

*skill regeneration_of_purpose [Rare] "Regeneration of Purpose VIII" 0

  [b]Cooldown:[/b] 1 day.

*skill polyglot [Uncommon] "Polyglot III" 0
