[b]Synopsis:[/b] The [cyan]System[/cyan] appeared overnight and changed everything, allowing humanity to reach heights considered impossible before. However, this gift came with a steep cost: monsters flooded the surface of the world, and eventually humanity retreated underground. You are one of the last three survivors on the entire planet, and together, you're building something impossible: a machine that might give the world a second chance.

Your choices will shape who you are. There are no wrong answers, though some paths are much shorter, and more brutal, than others.

*comment ── Section 2: System Blocks

*system
[ TUTORIAL: SYSTEM NOTIFICATIONS ]
*end_system

Throughout the story you'll see blocks like this one. These are [cyan]System notifications[/cyan]: in-world messages from the framework that governs stats, skills, and survival. They carry important information such as warnings, level changes, and status updates.

*system
WARNING: Seismic activity detected.
Protective ward integrity holding at 94%.
Mana reserve: 94,000 / 100,000.
*end_system

You'll also see brief popup messages appear and fade at the top of your screen. These confirm things like stat changes or items received.

*page_break

*comment ── Section 3: Stats Panel

*system
[ TUTORIAL: YOUR STATS PANEL ]
*end_system

Located at the top right of your screen is the Status button that opens your [cyan]Stats Panel[/cyan]. Check it whenever you want to see your current level, Essence, health, mana, and your three core attributes: [cyan]Body[/cyan], [cyan]Mind[/cyan], and [cyan]Spirit[/cyan]. You may also view your inventory, skills, achievements, journal, and access the item/skill store as well. 

[cyan]Essence[/cyan] is both your XP and the world's universal currency. Spend it in the [cyan]System Store[/cyan] (accessible from the Skills or Inventory tabs) to buy new abilities and items.

Some choices are gated behind stats or skills. If you don't meet a requirement, you'll sometimes see the option but won't be able to select it. That's the game's way of telling you what to work toward.
